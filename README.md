# ✍️ Risenglish: Online English School

[![Project Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Finished-blue)](https://github.com/seu-usuario/seu-repositorio)


> An online platform to manage English classes for Risenglish school with a focus on transparency and efficiency.

## Key Features

"Risenglish" offers a complete management ecosystem:

### Teacher/Administrator 
* **Class Managements:** Registration, editing and management of classes.
* **Content Management:** Registration, editing and management of materials used.
* **Recommendations Management** Registration, editing and management of links to other platforms that may be useful for learning.


### Student 
* **Class Access:** Students can view the class schedule and access the platform links.
* **Content Access:** Students can access and download the resources provided by teachers.

## Prerequisites

* [PHP] (version 7.4 or higher)
* [MySQL/MariaDB]
* [Composer]  (To manage PHP dependencies)
* Web Server (Apache or Nginx, or use PHP's built-in server)
